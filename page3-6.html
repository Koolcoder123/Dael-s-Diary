Entry 3: Xadia Spoke and I Listened


Xadia spoke to me once again; this time I understood her. I was told to travel some sites of the world, and I did.



          I had been told to travel, like I intended to before, and I did. Why was I traveling? Not sure. I wasn't told, but I think I know why. To visit nations of the present and the past.


          I first went to the Thurfir Caliphate, home of the Solmerite people. My father had said plenty regarding them; I don't have much to say against them. I was greeted by a relatively aggressive Solmerite, who tried to trap me in a fall trap. Not sure if it's a trick they show to visitors, but from the looks of it I could have died. It's odd; these are the people who have sold out not only humanity, but the world of Lumenor to an invading force (Netharia). I wonder what went through their heads. From the history I've seen, it seemed like the most beneficial stance they could have taken during the early phases of the global conflict. But again, they took advantage of what Xadia gave to them.
